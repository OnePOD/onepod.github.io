Welcome to OnePOD!

One of the many beautiful things is to look at a beatiful picture every day. OnePOD is an app to make your "look at a beautiful picture every day" task simpler with one button click.

Simple, and beautiful.

Supported Site(s)

We are currently only supporting National Geographic Photo of the Day. But more is coming!

Supported Platform(s)

We are currently only supporting Windows PC. But more is coming!

Authors and Contributors

OnePOD currently is an one-man-creaw team @OnePod. All the source code is open, so any one is welcome to check it out, or evern better, check something in!

Support or Contact

Having trouble with OnePOD? Or simply want to drop a note? Check out the documentation at One POD wiki, submit a bug report or feature request at One POD issues, or simply email me directly.
