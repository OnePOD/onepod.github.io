Welcome to OnePOD!

One of the many beautiful things is to look at a beautiful picture every day. OnePOD is an app to make your "look at a beautiful picture every day" task simpler with one button click.

Simple, and beautiful.

Supported Site(s)

We are currently only supporting National Geographic Photo of the Day. But more is coming!

Supported Platform(s)

We are currently only supporting Windows PC/Desktop. But more is coming!

Authors and Contributors

OnePOD currently is a personal open source project on GitHub. Any one is welcome to check it out, give me some advice on either coding or function, or even better, check something in!
